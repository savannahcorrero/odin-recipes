Pumpkin Pancakes

These Pancakes Will Give 'Em Pumpkin to Talk About!

Pumpkin pancakes are a cozy and delicious breakfast treat that's perfect for fall mornings or any time you're in the mood for something sweet and satisfying. These fluffy pancakes are made with real pumpkin puree and warm spices, creating a delightful combination of flavors that will warm you from the inside out. Plus, they're easy to make and can be whipped up in no time, making them a great choice for a lazy Sunday brunch or a weekday breakfast on the go. So, grab your spatula and let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • cooking spray

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir together milk, pumpkin, egg, oil, and vinegar in a large bowl until well combined.
  3. Whisk together flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, allspice, cinnamon, ginger, and salt in a separate bowl. Add to pumpkin mixture; whisk just until combined.
  4. Spray a griddle or frying pan with cooking spray; heat over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour 3 to 4 tablespoons batter for each pancake onto the hot griddle and flatten batter slightly with a spoon
  6. Cook until small bubbles appear, about 2 minutes.
  7. Flip and cook until golden brown, about 2 more minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
