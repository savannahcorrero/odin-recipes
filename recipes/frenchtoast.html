French Toast

Let's Get This Bread... And Make It French!

French toast is a classic breakfast dish that has been enjoyed for centuries. It's a simple and satisfying recipe that can be made with just a few basic ingredients, making it a perfect choice for a lazy weekend morning or a quick weekday breakfast. The combination of fluffy bread, rich custard, and warm spices creates a comforting and indulgent breakfast experience that is sure to satisfy. So, whether you're a seasoned breakfast pro or a beginner in the kitchen, grab a loaf of bread, some eggs, and let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot.
